      Help
      From Wikisocion

      There are a lot of people spread out across the globe who are interested in socionics. Here is a map of many of them.

      In this section we will keep track of the locations of meetings, clubs, and seminars. Please add new information as necessary. New cities can be added to the list by entering them in the search bar in the left column and choosing the "create page" option. Then add [[Category:Meetings]] to the bottom of the new page to add it to this list.
