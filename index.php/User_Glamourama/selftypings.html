      User:Glamourama/selftypings

      From Wikisocion
      < User:Glamourama

      This page is a listing of the socionics self-typings of the currently active members of the English-speaking Socionics community (which includes but is not necessarily limited to The16types.info, Socionix, and Socionics Workshop.) Currently, the users listed here have been at least somewhat active within the community within the last six months of the most recent update, give or take.

      Please do not make any edits to this page yourself. If you notice any errors, if you can contribute any missing information, or if you need me to update your own self-typing, please let me know on the discussion page, in the related thread at The16types.info forum, or by sending 'glamourama' a private message at The16types.info, Socionix, or Socionics Workshop.
