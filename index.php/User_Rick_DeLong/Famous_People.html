      Introduction

      As of 2011 this page will be structured very differently than before:

      1. Famous people will first be grouped into categories and subcategories. Some people may appear more than once. The category links lead to pages where more discussion of typings will take place. Comment on typings there!
      2. "!" means I am pretty certain, "?" means I have an opinion, and no mark means I think.
      3. For the time being I am concerned only about getting the typings posted once again. Argumentation may or may not be provided in the future.

      Important!

      1. I don't know how to make this any clearer — typings with a "?" do not indicate a strong opinion and can be changed easily. Only typings with a ! represent a strong opinion. Do not misrepresent my claimed degree of confidence. People with an "?" are here so that I remember that I have had an opinion or idea about them. This list is for personal reference just as much as it is for public display.
