      Intuitive logical extravert (Don Quixote)

      I am not like the others.
      Once upon a time, long long ago - as if it was yesterday - at dawn, from the gates of his house, rode out a rider on an old nag and a cardboard helmet on his head high. And stopped.
      The sun rose over the chain of distant mountains. The new day, like all the previous ones, was promising to be hot.
      The silence was so sleepy that it seemed that life itself has fallen asleep.
      The whole world was in full view. All roads were waiting for him ...
      ... Flick of reins ... first kick of a hoof spurred a cloud of golden dust ... BA-BOOM! - And the silence was broken with a roar, like crystal, and the world began to move again, again spun galaxies, the South Atlantic Gulf Stream was once again gleaming with multi-colored jets of water, while sleepy people began to rub their surprised from long hibernation eyes.
      Later, for a long time they will swear at this idiot who woke them up, and won't be able to get back to sleep. A new day has started, a new life.

      And then he came up with such a strange idea, which has never occurred in none madman in the world

      If we try to trace the development of any phenomenon or objects created by man, then at the very beginning, at the source, will always find an idea, "a strange idea." And make no mistake - this idea first occurred to ILE.

      "Think" and "invent" for it is almost the same. In his fantasies, he lived a thousand different lives, imagining themselves in different situations.

      Is naturally can imagine yourself to be liked kinogeroya, reading a book, at some point, will stand on line and in your mind will continue the story in their own way. In those few seconds of complete detachment from reality, he can live a decent life segment invented and when wakes up, puzzled for a moment - in what is the reality of it now?

      By and large, in order to fly away into the fantasy, he had the insignificance trifle - casually abandoned companion word, drawing on asphalt, forms clouds. And - now just seems that ILE is still around. In fact, he may already be on a pirate sailing ship or on another planet.

      But instead of thinking about work, I began to think about the reason the seas and oceans: what are found in the seas, whales and sharks, why whales are so big, and that it would, if the whales were carried out on the ground and walked on streets, and where we would live if some whale destroyed our home.

      No wonder that at that moment he ignored the question addressed to him or examiner will travel a few extra stops on the tram. You see, this "around here and seychasnaya" routine for him - as a five-meter pool for dolphins can live, but what a life!

      However the result of this fantasy is not the only one absent-mindedness. This is - a way of understanding the world, especially its new features. Combined in his irrepressible imagination already known elements of reality, ILE creates a new reality, which opens exciting new prospects.

      Where are these giants? - Yes, here they are in front of you!

      Of a million crazy ideas that ILE can "give to the surface" of his life, at least one, but is sure to be a genius. But the point is that he does not judge which one. Unfortunately, the vast majority of the people around him, too, can not do it right away, but they can (under favorable conditions) did not give it to sink into oblivion. ILE itself is unlikely to translate their ideas into reality: it only says it is possible, it is useful, it is very necessary - and throws them somewhere, to someone. Let him personally they do not bring any money, but it is much more important that his ideas have not gone to market.

      My true vocation - is open and invent.

      His fantasy gives not only an understanding of the potential of all that surrounds him. It helps him in unusual cases that require a quick decision. When he is not in personal experience, comes to the rescue "experience" invented thousands of lives. ILE instantly versed in them and finds almost the best way out of a critical situation. As to why he did just that - and then be happy to explain their actions in advance and not thought out.

      However the determining factor is accumulated knowledge. If he has the baggage is not big, and the intuition of little help. Yes, ILE is capable of self-examination to find previously unknown to him any proof of the theorem, but even the greatest genius would not be able to do this without the knowledge of mathematics.

      Better to rely on intuition rather than on common sense

      It may seem that ILE shares his real and invented lives. And sometimes it does happen, but in this case it is completely out in the fantasy world, forever leaving something nasty to him the reality. This is a case of "problem" ILE. If he is good, he takes their fancy just as different facets of real life. Yes, he loves his ideas, but also a very real respect for life in all its manifestations. He likes to watch her and make conclusions based on the facts that are found in his way, or rather, that give the people around him - he has some indirect way of looking at life. So he likes to listen to people and their judgment about everyone and everything.

      Nature often does not regret for ILE no memory, no insight. Even the most fleeting fact is not forgotten them, and at the right time often returns an ingenious idea. It can be very witty, and his word sharper swords and blasting dynamite. Able to ignite controversy literally from nothing. For some, the process of dispute ILE it more interesting result. In this case, be prepared for the fact that he will not agree to any practically your statement, and if you're lucky enough compelling reasons "priperetsya him to the wall," with confidence: "I said it!"

      In ILE highly developed logic and analytical skills. Based on the appearance of minor facts can make far-reaching conclusions. He is in a constant endeavor to establish connections between any of the phenomena of life ... and then, sometimes recklessly, share them with someone who first comes handy. Loves it when these abilities are recognized and valued by others.

      Can not spare time and energy to ensure that it is better to learn to do root cause analysis. He does not like people with "sloppy" style of thinking. According to him, everything in the world to be logical.

      Monotonous, tedious work, not paperwork for ILE, at any age, he plays in the hobby. Work will start if he could turn it into a game. He was seriously obey any discipline and can not stand when he is required unquestioning obedience instruction.

      I can not stand organically, when I try to squeeze in a vise discipline!

      In any case tends to bring something new, constantly improvising and her plans. If there is not a person who can gently and unobtrusively withhold his dashing aside and throwing from side to side - to fill up everything what only dotyanet. And right there in the ruins will build something new and interesting. His eternal creativity have time to stop ("All that is enough"), because he does not know the steps.

      ILE is engaged in that interesting, but not that profitable. Information that interested him, "seizes on the fly", but not always enough, for example, to be successful, hence the disruption to their studies. From the point of view of a child of this type of teacher employed only by constantly "chew" is that all long understood.

      Well versed in the ways of achieving any goals, but this seems to have it only in order to give good advice to others. For the very same myself usually nothing intelligent to think not. Comes a problem - and then it will be solved but somehow advance to prepare for it or try to prevent it is unlikely to be.

      Its efficiency and enterprise given to someone else's disposal, so that he will try other better than themselves. For ILE can always turn to the business problems, even just a hint, and it will not immediately write out just what or how, but he will also do everything. It will help so long until he sees that people are able to cope with everything else without interference.

      In his promises sometimes not necessary. If he says, "Okay, okay," it means (in most cases) that he can do what promised, but not the fact that he would certainly do it. Circumstances change - and change his intentions.

      Time to communicate does not regret not interrupt a friendly party because of some cases (if the matter promptly, can arrange so that it itself over to the right for a moment.)

      Because of the distraction and nesobrannosti might be late for the meeting. But try to find (or invent) a compelling reason to delay. He does not like to be taught to properly allocate your time or ask to be prudent.

      Functional description

      The first function: Intuition opportunities

      And the ability to grasp the essence of things and phenomena. Generating ideas, understanding their potential and possibilities of implementation. The ability to recreate the whole on some parts. Intelligence, good imagination, spontaneity, quick response in emergency situations, confusion in the home.

      The second function: the logic of relations

      The desire to find a logical connection between any of the facts. Ability to conduct root cause analysis, to separate the important from the unimportant, to be logical. Tendency to the exact sciences, the desire to explain everything from the standpoint of logic.

      The third function: volitional sensing

      Striving to create an idea of ​​how strong-willed and determined person. Analysis of the actions of others in terms of the manifestation of their willpower. Accumulation of their experiences. Internal diffidence. Self-criticism and rejection of foreign criticism of his own integrity, commitment and breakdown qualities.

      Fourth function: the ethics of relations

      Lack of understanding of how people relate to each other and to him personally. Lack of understanding of their own desires. Categorical refusal "imposed" to someone with your feelings. Avoiding any talk about ethics, morality and tact, unwillingness to assess how their own actions and the actions of others in terms of morality. Or too acute sensitivity to his own unethical, or complete lack of tact and ethics. Resentment at "enemies", tactless, impolite.

      Fifth function: sensory experiences

      Weak sensory sensitivity. Incompetence in the establishment of comfort. Thanks for showing the touch of care of yourself and your comfort. Trust in the opinion of the other people on resettlement life. Resentment at life in the event of insufficient comfort of their own existence. Passivity, confusion, untidy.

      Sixth function: the ethics of emotions

      The desire to be cheerful and energetic. Weak ability to independently deal with a bad mood and apathy. Periodic melancholy and fatigue. Emotional support from loved ones is accepted with gratitude. Subconscious expectation on their part to be commended for their own good mood. Love the holidays, a little deliberate gaiety.

      Seventh function: the intuition of time

      Ability if necessary to structure their and other people's time. Unconscious sense of time. Unwillingness to take advice and recommendations on the timing of their actions. Rejection of foreign warnings of possible dangers. Distribution of their advice and warnings to close people. Unwillingness of others to fail over time. Punctuality (or sverhpunktualnost), does not notice the danger in their path.

      Eighth function: the business logic

      Subliminal track all issues related to business. Stubborn and hidden from the attention of others the desire to be always in the know of other people. Constant tacit suggestion of his business skills. Willingness to come to their aid. Discreet and nevypyachivaemaya work. Complete and not always aware of the responsibility for their actions. Unwillingness to harm people in their affairs. Waiting for them silent gratitude for their service. Altruism, belittling the value of their services.
